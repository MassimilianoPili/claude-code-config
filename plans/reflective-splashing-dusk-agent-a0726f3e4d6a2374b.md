# IAP + Agriturismo + Land Purchase in Sardinia -- Complete Guide

**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Epistemic status:** Practical administrative guidance based on current Italian law (D.Lgs. 99/2004, L.R. Sardegna 11/2015) and institutional sources (INPS, Invitalia, Regione Sardegna). Administrative details verified against official provincial/regional portals. Some timelines are approximate as they depend on local bureaucratic speed.

**Confidence:** Medium-High -- laws and procedures are stable; specific bando timelines and fund availability change yearly.

## 1. IAP Qualification Process

### Legal Basis

The IAP (Imprenditore Agricolo Professionale) is defined by **D.Lgs. 29 marzo 2004, n. 99, art. 1**, which replaced the old IATP (Imprenditore Agricolo a Titolo Principale) from D.Lgs. 228/2001.

### Three Cumulative Requirements

To qualify as IAP, you must satisfy ALL THREE simultaneously:

| Requirement | Threshold | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| **Time dedication** | >= 50% of own working time dedicated to agricultural activity | Reduced to 25% in mountain/disadvantaged areas (many Sardinian zones qualify) |
| **Income** | >= 50% of total income derived from agricultural activity | Reduced to 25% in mountain/disadvantaged areas |
| **Professional competence** | One of: (a) degree/diploma in agriculture, (b) 150-hour IAP training course, (c) 3+ years documented experience running an agricultural enterprise | Training courses in Sardinia: Agenzia Laore (free, periodic), iForm (~700 EUR), SOS.OR (~700 EUR), various private providers |

(Source: D.Lgs. 99/2004 art. 1, comma 1; consulenzaagricola.it 2025; Confagricoltura)

**CRITICAL -- Legge di Bilancio 2026 change (L. 199/2025, art. 1 commi 15-16):** New modifications to IAP/CD fiscal requirements effective from 2026 budget year. Verify current text before proceeding.

### Where to Apply in Sardinia

In Sardinia, the IAP qualification is issued by the **Provincia** (or Citta Metropolitana) of residence:

| Territory | Office |
|-----------|--------|
| Area Cagliari | Citta Metropolitana di Cagliari -- Settore Ambiente |
| Provincia di Nuoro | Provincia di Nuoro -- Servizio Agricoltura |
| Provincia di Sassari | Provincia di Sassari |
| Provincia di Oristano | Provincia di Oristano |
| Provincia Sud Sardegna | Provincia del Sud Sardegna |

The qualification is registered in the **SIAR** (Sistema Informativo Agricolo Regionale -- Piattaforma Regionale degli Imprenditori Agricoli Professionali).

(Source: cittametropolitanacagliari.it/riconoscimento_iap; provincia.nuoro.it)

### Documents Needed

1. **Domanda di riconoscimento IAP** (modulo provinciale)
2. **Certificato di iscrizione INPS** gestione CD/IAP (or proof of application)
3. **Attestato di competenza professionale**: diploma agrario, OR attestato corso 150 ore, OR dichiarazione sostitutiva di 3+ anni di esperienza
4. **Fascicolo aziendale AGEA** aggiornato (via CAA)
5. **Dichiarazione dei redditi** (ultimo anno o ultimi 3 anni) -- to prove the income threshold
6. **Visura camerale** (if already registered at Camera di Commercio as impresa agricola)
7. **Titolo di conduzione** dei terreni (proprieta, affitto, comodato)
8. **Piano colturale** or piano aziendale

### IAP Provvisorio (Provisional)

**Yes, it exists in Sardinia.** This is critical for your scenario.

From the Citta Metropolitana di Cagliari and Provincia di Nuoro pages:

> "Gli imprenditori agricoli che richiedono la qualifica di IAP ma che non sono in possesso di uno o piu requisiti al momento della presentazione della richiesta [possono ottenere IAP Provvisorio]"

**How it works:**
- You apply declaring that you DO NOT YET meet all 3 requirements
- The Provincia grants **IAP Provvisorio** (provisional)
- You have **24 mesi (2 years)** to fulfill the missing requirements (typically: income threshold, since you're just starting; or INPS registration)
- If you fulfill them within 24 months, it converts to **IAP Definitivo**
- If you fail, the qualification is revoked and any fiscal benefits must be repaid

**This is how you solve the chicken-and-egg problem**: you don't yet have land (so no agricultural income), but you need IAP to buy land with PPC benefits. IAP Provvisorio bridges this gap.

### Timeline

- **Corso IAP 150 ore**: 2-4 months (courses run periodically; iForm in Sardinia offers 140h online + 20h in-person)
- **INPS registration**: can be done immediately once you have land title (even preliminary contract)
- **IAP Provvisorio**: typically 30-60 days from application
- **IAP Definitivo**: after satisfying all requirements (up to 24 months from Provvisorio)

### Cost

| Item | Approximate Cost |
|------|-----------------|
| IAP training course (150h) | 500-800 EUR (private providers); FREE via Agenzia Laore (limited spots, periodic) |
| INPS annual contribution (CD/IAP) | ~2,500-4,500 EUR/year depending on fascia (see below) |
| Camera di Commercio registration | ~100-200 EUR (diritto annuale + MoCRA) |
| Provincia application fee | Free or minimal (marca da bollo 16 EUR) |

INPS contributions for CD/IAP are calculated on **reddito convenzionale** by fascia:

| Fascia | Reddito Convenzionale 2025 | Aliquota | Approx. Annual Contribution |
|--------|---------------------------|----------|----------------------------|
| 1a | 10,696.64 EUR (65.19 x 156gg) | 24% | ~2,567 EUR |

(Source: INPS Circolare 107/2025; Confagricoltura Bologna; edotto.com)

## 2. Agritourism Registration in Sardinia

### Current Law

The Sardinia agritourism law is **L.R. 11 maggio 2015, n. 11** "Norme in materia di agriturismo, ittiturismo, pescaturismo, fattoria didattica e sociale" -- this REPLACED the older L.R. 18/1998 which you mentioned. The 2015 law is the current governing legislation.

This was confirmed by the PSR Sardegna bando 6.4.1 (2024) which explicitly references "legge regionale 11 maggio 2015, n. 11."

The national framework is **Legge 20 febbraio 2006, n. 96** (Disciplina dell'agriturismo).

(Source: files.regione.sardegna.it bando 6.4.1-2024; sardegnaimpresa.eu TU Attivita Produttive 2023)

### What Qualifies as Agritourism Activity

Under L.R. 11/2015 and national law L. 96/2006, agritourism can include:

1. **Ospitalita** (accommodation): rooms, camping areas
2. **Ristorazione** (food service): using own production primarily
3. **Degustazione** (tasting): wine, oil, cheese, etc.
4. **Attivita ricreative, culturali e didattiche**: THIS IS YOUR KEY CATEGORY
   - Nature walks and guided botanical tours
   - Garden visits and landscape appreciation
   - Cultural workshops (tea ceremony can fit here)
   - Fattoria didattica (educational farm)
   - Wellness activities connected to rural environment
5. **Vendita diretta** (direct sale of farm products)

### Can a "Garden Park" Qualify?

**Yes, with careful framing.** The critical requirements are:

1. **Connessione con l'attivita agricola**: the garden/park activities must be "connesse e complementari" to the primary agricultural activity. They cannot be the SOLE activity.
2. **Prevalenza agricola**: agricultural revenue must be >= agritourism revenue (this is the "prevalence rule" -- art. 2135 CC activities must predominate)
3. **The garden must contain actual agricultural production**: ornamental plants alone likely do not qualify. But a landscape garden with:
   - Aromatic/medicinal herbs (lavender, rosemary, myrtle -- all native to Sardinia)
   - Fruit trees (olive, citrus, figs)
   - A small vineyard
   - Native Sardinian species used for herbal products

   ...this would create a legitimate agricultural production base that supports the "garden park" as an agritourism attraction.

### Tea Ceremony + Botanical Walks + Garden Visits

This combination fits perfectly under "attivita ricreative, culturali e didattiche" IF:

- You produce the tea ingredients yourself (herbal infusions from your garden -- myrtle, cistus, helichrysum, etc.)
- The botanical walks are on YOUR agricultural land
- The activities are complementary to, not substituting, your agricultural production

**Strategy**: frame it as "fattoria didattica + degustazione di infusi" rather than "tea ceremony" (which sounds non-agricultural). The substance is the same; the framing matters for the bureaucracy.

### Minimum Agricultural Production

There is no fixed minimum acreage or production volume in the law. However:

- The **prevalence rule** requires that agricultural time/revenue > agritourism time/revenue
- In practice, this means you need to actually cultivate the land and sell/transform products
- Even a small-scale operation (olive oil, honey, herbal products, essential oils) satisfies this if your agritourism activity is proportionally modest

### Registration Process

1. **Be IAP or CD** first (or at least registered as impresa agricola)
2. **Obtain nulla osta comunale** (building/zoning compliance for any structures)
3. **Present SCIA** (Segnalazione Certificata di Inizio Attivita) to the **Comune** via SUAPE (Sportello Unico Attivita Produttive ed Edilizia)
4. **ASL certification** (if offering food/drink -- HACCP compliance)
5. **Iscrizione nell'elenco regionale** degli operatori agrituristici (Regione Sardegna)
6. **Classification**: Sardinia uses a classification system (spighe/suns) based on services offered

**Timeline**: SCIA is immediate (30 days for administration to object). Full setup with ASL and classification: 2-4 months.

## 3. PPC Purchase -- Practical Steps

### The Core Question: IAP BEFORE or AFTER the Notary Deed?

**The rule (D.L. 194/2009, art. 2 comma 4-bis):**

The buyer must be:
- Coltivatore Diretto OR IAP
- **Iscritto nella gestione previdenziale e assistenziale** INPS

**AT THE TIME OF THE DEED.**

However, there is a crucial exception:

### Under-40 Exception (Legge di Bilancio 2023, art. 1 comma 110, L. 197/2022)

> "Dal primo gennaio 2023 le agevolazioni fiscali previste per la piccola proprieta contadina sono applicabili anche alle **persone fisiche di eta inferiore a quaranta anni** che dichiarino di voler ottenere, entro il termine di **ventiquattro mesi**, l'iscrizione nella gestione previdenziale e assistenziale prevista per i coltivatori diretti e gli imprenditori agricoli professionali (IAP)."

(Source: Notaio Tonalini, agevolazioni PPC 2025; altalex.com)

**This means**: if you are under 40, you CAN buy with PPC benefits NOW and get INPS registration within 24 months. The notary includes a specific declaration in the deed.

**If you are over 40**: you MUST be registered with INPS before the deed. In this case:
- Get IAP Provvisorio
- Register with INPS (they accept provisional IAP)
- Then go to the notary

### What the Notary Does

The PPC declaration is made **directly in the atto di compravendita**. The buyer declares:
- To be CD or IAP (or under-40 intending to become one within 24 months)
- To be registered (or intending to register) with INPS
- To commit to cultivating/managing the land for at least 5 years

No separate certificate from the Provincia is needed anymore (since 2010 reform -- Risoluzione AdE 36/E del 17 maggio 2010). The old "certificato provvisorio dell'ispettorato agrario" is abolished.

(Source: Notaio Tonalini; Risoluzione 36/E 2010)

### Tax Savings with PPC

| Tax | Without PPC | With PPC |
|-----|-------------|----------|
| Imposta di registro | 15% of price | Fixed (200 EUR) |
| Imposta ipotecaria | Fixed 50 EUR | Fixed (200 EUR) |
| Imposta catastale | Fixed 50 EUR | 1% of price |
| Imposta di bollo | Standard | Exempt |

**Example**: for a 100,000 EUR purchase:
- Without PPC: ~15,300 EUR in taxes
- With PPC: ~1,400 EUR in taxes
- **Savings: ~13,900 EUR**

### The 5-Year Commitment ("Vincolo quinquennale")

You **cannot** within 5 years of the deed:
1. Voluntarily sell the land
2. **Cease to cultivate or directly manage the land**

**Does maintaining a landscape garden count as "coltivazione"?**

Yes, IF:
- The garden includes actual agricultural crops (herbs, olives, fruit trees -- not purely ornamental)
- You are actively managing the land (documented via fascicolo aziendale updates, sales of products, INPS contributions)
- You maintain IAP/CD status for the full 5 years

**Decadenza (loss of benefits)**: if you violate the 5-year commitment, you must pay back the difference between PPC taxes and ordinary taxes, plus 30% penalty and interest.

## 4. PSR/CSR Sardegna Bandi -- How to Actually Apply

### The Current Program

The old "PSR" (Piano di Sviluppo Rurale) 2014-2020 is now replaced by the **CSR** (Complemento per lo Sviluppo Rurale) **Sardegna 2023-2027**, part of the national PSP (Piano Strategico PAC).

**Main portal**: https://sardegnapsr.it/ (still uses PSR branding)
**Managing authority**: Regione Autonoma della Sardegna, Assessorato Agricoltura
**Paying agency**: **ARGEA** (Agenzia Regionale per il Sostegno all'Agricoltura) -- this is the Sardinia-specific organismo pagatore, NOT AGEA directly. ARGEA interfaces with AGEA/SIAN.

(Source: sardegnapsr.it; agenziaargea.it)

### Relevant Measures for Your Project

| Intervention | Code | What it funds | Max amount |
|-------------|------|---------------|------------|
| Insediamento giovani agricoltori | **SRE01** (ex misura 6.1) | Lump sum for under-41 first-time farmers | Up to ~50,000 EUR |
| Investimenti in aziende agricole | **SRD01** (ex misura 4.1) | Farm investments (structures, machinery, plantings) | Variable, up to 60% co-financing |
| Diversificazione agriturismo | **SRD06** (ex misura 6.4.1) | Agritourism investments (building renovation, garden infrastructure) | Variable |

**SRE01 -- Insediamento Giovani Agricoltori** is probably the most relevant for you:

Requirements (from sardegnapsr.it):
- Age: 18-40 (under 41 not yet completed)
- First time setting up as capo azienda (head of farm)
- Adequate professional competence (IAP course counts)
- Present a Piano Aziendale (business plan)
- Acquire IAP qualification within 36 months from insediamento

### Fascicolo Aziendale at AGEA -- How to Create One

The **fascicolo aziendale** is the foundational document for ALL agricultural subsidies. It is created and managed through the **SIAN** (Sistema Informativo Agricolo Nazionale) portal.

**You do NOT create it yourself.** You go to a **CAA** (Centro Autorizzato di Assistenza Agricola):

Sardinia-based CAAs include:
- **Coldiretti** (largest network)
- **Confagricoltura**
- **CIA** (Confederazione Italiana Agricoltori)
- **Copagri**
- **CAA Liberi Agricoltori**

**Steps:**
1. Go to any CAA office in Sardinia
2. Bring: codice fiscale, documento identita, titolo di conduzione terreni (or preliminary contract), visura catastale of the land
3. The CAA creates your fascicolo aziendale on SIAN
4. The fascicolo contains: your data, land parcels, crops declared, livestock if any
5. Once the fascicolo exists, you can submit domande di sostegno (subsidy applications)

**Cost**: CAA services are often free (they are compensated by AGEA) or charge a modest fee (50-100 EUR).

### Application Process for a Bando

1. **Bando published** on sardegnapsr.it and BURAS (Bollettino Ufficiale Regione Sardegna)
2. **Check requirements** -- each bando has specific eligibility criteria
3. **Prepare documentation** -- piano aziendale, fascicolo aggiornato, business plan
4. **Submit domanda di sostegno** through **portale SIAN** (www.sian.it) -- this requires SPID or CIE authentication
5. **ARGEA evaluates** and publishes graduatoria (ranking)
6. **If approved**: sign the commitment, execute the plan
7. **Request payment**: submit domanda di pagamento with documentation of expenses
8. **ARGEA verifies and pays**

### Timeline (Typical)

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Bando open for applications | 30-90 days |
| Evaluation and graduatoria | 3-6 months |
| Communication of approval | 1-2 months after graduatoria |
| Plan execution | 12-24 months (depends on bando) |
| Payment request and verification | 3-6 months |
| **Total from bando to money in account** | **12-24 months minimum** |

### Can You Hire a Consulente?

**Absolutely yes**, and it is strongly recommended. Typical options:

- **Agronomo** (agronomist) -- for the Piano Aziendale and technical documentation
- **CAA** -- for fascicolo management and domanda submission
- **Consulente PSR specializzato** -- private consultants who specialize in PSR applications

**Cost**: typically 5-15% of the funded amount, or a fixed fee (1,000-5,000 EUR depending on complexity). Some charge only on success.

## 5. Resto al Sud -- Detailed Eligibility

### CRITICAL UPDATE: Resto al Sud 1.0 is CLOSED

> "A partire dalle ore 24.00 del **14 ottobre 2025** non sara piu consentita la presentazione delle domande di agevolazione a valere su Resto al Sud (DL 91/2017)"

(Source: invitalia.it, retrieved 2026-03-23)

### Resto al Sud 2.0 is ACTIVE

**Resto al Sud 2.0** launched on **15 ottobre 2025**, replacing the original program. It has DIFFERENT rules.

(Source: invitalia.it/incentivi-e-strumenti/resto-al-sud-20)

### Eligibility Requirements -- Resto al Sud 2.0

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Age** | **18-35** (under 36 at application date) -- NOTE: this is much lower than the old program which was under-56 |
| **Residence** | Must be resident in eligible territories (all of Sardinia qualifies as Mezzogiorno) OR willing to transfer residence within specific deadline |
| **Employment status** | Must be NEET or unemployed or inactive (not employed at application time) |
| **No existing business** | Must not be titolare of an active impresa at time of application |
| **Not already beneficiary** of other national startup incentives | Cannot cumulate with other Invitalia measures |

**KEY CHANGE from 1.0**: the age limit dropped from 55 to **35**. If you are over 35, Resto al Sud 2.0 is NOT available.

### Can You Apply If Not Yet Resident in Sardinia?

**Yes**, but you must transfer residence to an eligible territory within a specific timeline (typically before first disbursement or within 60 days of approval). Sardinia is fully eligible as part of the Mezzogiorno.

### Funding Structure -- Resto al Sud 2.0

The structure changed significantly from 1.0:

- **Total budget**: 356.4 million EUR
- **Max per applicant**: specific limits per category (to be confirmed -- the old program was up to 200,000 EUR for companies, 60,000 EUR for individuals)
- **100% fondo perduto** (grant, no loan component) -- this is a major change from 1.0 which was 50% grant + 50% subsidized loan
- **Form**: voucher-based system (reimbursement of documented expenses)

### Best Business Type

For an agritourism project:
- **Impresa individuale** is simplest but caps may be lower
- **SRL semplificata** offers liability protection and potentially higher caps
- **Societa agricola** if you want the IAP benefits to apply at corporate level

Invitalia evaluates within **90 days** from application.

### Timeline

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Application preparation | 1-2 months |
| Invitalia evaluation | Up to 90 days |
| Approval notification | ~30 days |
| Business setup (if not already done) | 60 days from approval |
| Expense execution + voucher claim | 9-12 months |
| **Total to first disbursement** | **8-15 months** |

## Recommended Sequence of Actions

Based on all the above, here is the optimal step-by-step path:

### Phase 0 -- Preparation (Month 1-3)
1. **Enroll in IAP 150h course** -- start immediately (iForm, SOS.OR, or Agenzia Laore if available)
2. **Identify the land** you want to buy
3. **Contact a CAA** (Coldiretti or CIA in Sardinia) -- they become your operational partner for everything agricultural
4. **Contact a notaio** to discuss the purchase timeline and PPC applicability
5. **Verify your age**: if under 40, you have the PPC under-40 exception; if under 36, Resto al Sud 2.0 is available

### Phase 1 -- Legal Setup (Month 3-6)
6. **Complete IAP course** (get attestato)
7. **Apply for IAP Provvisorio** at your Provincia
8. **Register with INPS** gestione CD/IAP (the CAA can help)
9. **Open fascicolo aziendale** at CAA/SIAN
10. **Register impresa agricola** at Camera di Commercio (Registro Imprese, sezione speciale)

### Phase 2 -- Land Purchase (Month 4-8)
11. **Compromesso** (preliminary purchase contract) -- this can happen before IAP definitivo
12. If under 40: **proceed to rogito (deed) with PPC** using the 24-month declaration
13. If over 40: wait for INPS registration confirmation, then proceed to rogito with PPC
14. **Update fascicolo aziendale** with the new land parcels

### Phase 3 -- Agritourism Setup (Month 6-18)
15. **Prepare Piano Aziendale** with an agronomo -- include: agricultural production plan (herbs, olives, etc.) + agritourism activities (garden visits, botanical walks, herbal tasting)
16. **Apply for CSR/PSR bandi** as they open (check sardegnapsr.it regularly):
    - SRE01 if under 41 (insediamento giovani)
    - SRD01 for farm investments
    - SRD06 for agritourism diversification
17. **If eligible, apply for Resto al Sud 2.0** (if under 36)
18. **File SCIA** for agritourism at the Comune

### Phase 4 -- Operations (Month 12+)
19. **Start agricultural activity** (planting, first harvests)
20. **Open agritourism** (garden visits, botanical walks, herbal infusions)
21. **Document everything** for the 5-year PPC commitment
22. **Convert IAP Provvisorio to Definitivo** within 24 months

## Key Risks and Warnings

1. **Age is the biggest variable**: under 36 opens Resto al Sud 2.0; under 40 opens PPC under-40 exception and PSR SRE01; over 40 makes everything harder (no Resto al Sud, no PPC grace period, no insediamento giovani)

2. **IAP income requirement**: if you have other significant income (e.g., IT salary), the 50% agricultural income threshold may be hard to meet for IAP definitivo. In disadvantaged areas (many Sardinian zones), the threshold drops to 25% -- verify if your target area qualifies.

3. **Prevalenza agricola for agritourism**: your agritourism revenue cannot exceed your agricultural revenue. Start agricultural production FIRST before scaling up tourism activities.

4. **PPC decadenza**: if you sell or stop cultivating within 5 years, the tax penalty is severe (~14% of purchase price + 30% surcharge + interest).

5. **Bandi timing**: PSR/CSR bandi are NOT continuous. They open periodically. If you miss a window, the next one may be 6-12 months away. The CAA monitors this for you.

6. **Resto al Sud 2.0 may run out**: with 356.4M EUR budget and high demand, the program could exhaust funds before you apply. Apply early if eligible.

## Serendipitous Connections

**Bradley-Terry / Preference Learning (Ranking Todo project)**: the PSR bando evaluation uses a point-based ranking system (graduatoria) with weighted criteria. The weights assigned to different eligibility factors (young farmer, disadvantaged area, organic farming, etc.) are essentially a linear scoring model. An interesting comparison could be made with how preference learning models aggregate multi-criteria decisions -- the PSR scoring is a fixed-weight additive model, while Bradley-Terry would learn weights from pairwise comparisons. If the Regione wanted to optimize bando criteria allocation, a preference-learning approach could be more efficient than the current fixed-weight system.
